Protecting Your Privacy

We use anonymous analytics to understand how the MCLC Client is used and to improve our services and stability. No personal or identifiable data is linked to your browsing sessions or launcher usage.

What data do we collect?
The launcher transmits strictly anonymous technical data, such as:

  • Selected Mod Loader (e.g., Fabric, NeoForge)
  • Minecraft Version played
  • Operating System (Windows, Linux, macOS)
  • Assigned RAM
  • Basic hardware info (e.g., Java version)

You can opt-out of this anonymous tracking at any time directly within the MCLC Launcher settings.
